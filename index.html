What To Expect

Scale

125+ exhibiting companies from St. Louis and beyond.

Variety

Exhibitors from all aspects of the music industry, including music photography, music-related art, music/performance-related non-profits, and more.

Expanse

Nearly 40,000 square feet of exhibition space spread across two buildings.

Interactivity

Interactive exhibits to fully experience a wide variety of handmade musical instruments, amplifiers, and effects.

Experiences

A fully working recording studio, featuring some of St. Louis' best engineers and musicians, open to all attendees.

Entertainment

Food trucks and live music outside in the Cortex Commons.

The Expo Is The Accelerator

The Tritone Expo's Mission Rests On Three Main Pillars

Entrepreneurship

Fostering innovative local and regional businesses that are not only making products, but are also creating new ideas and avenues for the music industry in general.

Economy

Creating and promoting a marketplace that elevates sales for local and regional businesses that produce hand made gear, as well as our sponsors and partners that share are passion for music.

Education

Promoting St. Louis as a hub for music education, which it has been steeped in for decades, not just at the university level with top rated programs that produce working musicians and engineers, but also at the beginning level, literally writing the book on how to play music.
